SPIN Fellowship

Explore our global educational program in pediatric neuroimaging designed for early-career radiologists.

Fellowship of the Society of Pediatric Neuroimaging

A structured program delivered in three distinct parts:

The first two are comprehensive virtual courses offered annually — “Foundations” and “Levelling Up” — designed to cover all key aspects of pediatric neuroimaging through expert-led instruction.

The final segment is an in-person program, conducted in select global regions including the Far East, India, Middle East, Europe, and the Americas. While the virtual and in-person modules may be attended separately, both must be completed within a two-year period to fulfill the full program cycle.
