SPIN Membership

Unlock Your Potential

SPIN is an incredible professional society comprised of clinicians, researchers, and allied health professionals from across the globe, all united by their common passion for pediatric neuroimaging. As the world's first international platform for our dynamic subspecialty, we have held successful annual meetings since our inception in 2018.

Revolutionary member-benefits include world-class educational outreach, collaborative research, and 1-1 mentorship from our Executive Committee. There is no better way to augment and accelerate your practice.
