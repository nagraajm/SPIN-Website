The Future of Pediatric Neuroimaging

Join the Society of Pediatric Neuroimaging (SPIN) for a groundbreaking in-person conference in Dubai, 07–09 November 2025, in partnership with the Radiology Society of Emirates.

The annual SPIN conference this year in Dubai is designed to train bright minds like you further in the art and skill of Neuroradiology. Our mission is international outreach with world class teachers and our vision is to ensure we do our bit to make the care of patients with complex neurological disorders as standardised as possible across the world. This year, SPIN returns with an enhanced offering, with a nice mix of both pediatric and adult topics, kept practical and attuned to daily practice.

Forget passive lectures—this is interactive learning at its best! Our expert faculty don’t just teach—they engage, stepping into the room to dissect complex cases with you. Through immersive workshops, you’ll tackle real-world neuroradiology challenges side-by-side with pioneers in the field, turning theory into actionable skills. It isn’t just education—it’s collaboration, debate, and discovery, all designed to sharpen your clinical edge.

This year we collaborate with the Radiology Society of Emirates, and The Cortex Club to make your learning experience truly comprehensive, and we hope to build a platform for a strongly mentoring social environment.

Why SPIN Dubai 2025 Stands Out !

World-Class Mentorship

Learn directly from 50+ global experts
- no sterile lectures, just interactive, case-based learning.

Standardized Care

Our mission is to elevate neurological care worldwide through hands-on workshops and real-world applications.

Cutting-Edge Topics

Explore AI in neuroimaging, advanced fMRI techniques, radiogenomics, fetal MRI, and post-mortem insights.

Collaborative Learning

Partnering with Radiology Society of Emirates & The Cortex Club for a mentorship-rich, socially engaging experience.

Multidisciplinary Panels

Collaborate with experts in tumor boards, epilepsy, metabolics, and stroke interventions.

Unique Networking

Sunrise meditations, interactive labs, and the Fellowship Viva Vocé that challenge traditional conference models.

Who Should Attend and Why?

Pediatric radiologists, neuroradiologists, clinicians, and researchers seeking to elevate their skills with pragmatic, globally relevant education. SPIN isn’t just a conference—it’s a two-way knowledge exchange where you debate, question, and grow alongside pioneers in the field.
