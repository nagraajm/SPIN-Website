About the Annual SPIN Meeting

The annual SPIN conference is designed to train bright minds like you further in the art and skill of Pediatric Neuroradiology. Our mission is international outreach with world class teachers and our vision is to ensure we do our bit to make the care of children with complex neurological disorders as standardised as possible across the world.

The contents of the course are designed with great care to bring to focus all key topics in a workshop based format, where the teacher is not on a stage before you in a sterile conference environment, but mingles with the learners directly to discuss complex cases with strong learning messages. The knowledge transfer is therefore a 2 way process.

The syllabus covered gives you practical skills for day to day work and provides a unique perspective to all your background reading. We also focus immensely on adult correlations for core pediatric topics. Below you will find the clusters of learning we focus on.

Any questions, please feel free to ask us.
