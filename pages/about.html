President's Welcome

Welcome to SPIN Academics

Dear Colleagues and Friends,

It is my great pleasure to welcome you to the Society for Pediatric and Investigative Neuroradiology (SPIN). Our organization stands at the forefront of pediatric neuroradiology education, research, and professional development.

SPIN was founded with a vision to create a global community of pediatric neuroradiology professionals dedicated to advancing the field through innovative education, collaborative research, and evidence-based practice.

Whether you are a seasoned professional, a fellow in training, or someone interested in pediatric neuroradiology, SPIN offers valuable resources and connections to support your professional journey.

Together, we can continue to advance pediatric neuroradiology and improve patient care worldwide.
