President's Welcome

It is a privilege to serve as President of the SPIN Neuroradiology Society, a dynamic community dedicated to advancing pediatric neuroradiology. I am honored to build on its strong foundation and collaborative spirit.

My sincere gratitude to Dr. Kish Mankad for his leadership in shaping SPIN. The success of SPIN2024 in Goa highlighted our collective commitment to high-quality education and meaningful discussions.

Looking ahead, SPIN2025 in Dubai, in collaboration with the Radiological Society of the Emirates, marks an exciting milestone. By bringing the event to the UAE, we aim to expand access and engagement across the region.

Education remains central to SPIN. We will continue growing our web-based learning platforms and fellowship programs to shape the next generation of pediatric neuroradiologists.

None of this is possible without our dedicated team and members. Thank you for your contributions—I welcome your ideas as we push boundaries and elevate pediatric neuroradiology together.

Warm regards,

Dr Ozgur Oztekin
