Virtual Neuroradiology Clinics

A global collaborative series by The Cortex Club and the Society of Pediatric Neuroimaging—designed for knowledge-sharing, case-solving, and advancing virtual diagnostics.

Learning Objectives

  • Diagnose with Confidence: Identify and differentiate a range of CNS conditions (tumors, strokes, trauma) in both adults and children.
  • Master Imaging Modalities: Interpret MRI, CT, MRA & DSA scans for precise virtual diagnosis.
  • Evaluate Interventional Techniques: Discuss endovascular therapy, spinal procedures, and biopsies in a virtual planning setting.
  • Strengthen Multidisciplinary Insight: Collaborate virtually with global experts to optimize patient care.
  • Harness AI & Tech: Apply emerging tech like advanced imaging tools and telemedicine.

Real-World Case-Based Learning

“Neuroradiology Dilemmas” – Rapid-fire case presentations followed by collaborative brainstorming.

  • 🧑‍⚕️ Open to: Radiologists, Neurosurgeons, Neurologists, Psychiatrists, Pediatricians, Residents & Consultants.
  • 💡 Format: 10-minute case presentations, max 10 slides.
  • 💬 Followed by: Focused group discussion
  • 🧠 Pediatric & Adult Neuroradiology
  • 👂 Head & Neck, Neurointervention

Submit your case: virtualneuroradiologyclinics@gmail.com
